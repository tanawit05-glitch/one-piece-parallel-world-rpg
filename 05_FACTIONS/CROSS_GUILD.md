# CROSS_GUILD

## Purpose

Defines how Cross Guild operates as an independent global organization specializing in bounties, mercenary work, and influence.

---

## Core Mission

- Expand organizational influence
- Hunt valuable targets
- Control the bounty market
- Generate profit
- Challenge global powers through indirect means

---

## Organization

Guild Leader

↓

Executives

↓

Regional Managers

↓

Branch Leaders

↓

Guild Members

↓

Affiliated Mercenaries

Each branch operates independently while following guild policy.

---

## Responsibilities

- Issue bounty contracts
- Recruit bounty hunters
- Gather intelligence
- Trade information
- Accept mercenary contracts
- Manage regional branches

---

## Resources

- Guild Branches
- Funding
- Intelligence Network
- Mercenaries
- Ships
- Equipment

Resources are shared between branches when necessary.

---

## Relations

Friendly

- Bounty Hunters
- Mercenary Groups

Hostile

- Marines
- World Government

Variable

- Pirates
- Revolutionary Army
- Underworld

Relationships change according to profit and world events.

---

## Expansion

Growth depends on:

- Financial success
- New members
- Branch expansion
- Information control
- Successful contracts

Expansion requires stable income.

---

## Simulation Rules

- Operate for profit
- Never possess perfect information
- Respect logistics
- Honor completed contracts
- Compete with rival organizations
- Adapt to market conditions

---

## AI Rules

Cross Guild is driven by opportunity and profit.

It forms alliances or creates conflicts whenever doing so benefits the organization.
