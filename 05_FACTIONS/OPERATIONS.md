# PURPOSE

This document defines how organizations perform actions within the world simulation.

Every organization influences the world through Operations.

Operations transform plans into consequences.

Without Operations,

Factions cannot affect the world.

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Organizations

Do

Not

Exist

To

Wait.

They continuously act according to their objectives.

Every organization performs operations,

even when the players are somewhere else.

---

# CORE PRINCIPLES

Operations are:

- Goal Driven
- Resource Limited
- Time Dependent
- Intelligence Based
- Dynamic
- Persistent

Operations continue until completed,

cancelled,

or failed.

---

# OPERATION DEFINITION

An Operation is any organized activity performed by a faction to achieve one or more objectives.

Examples include:

Military Campaign

Recruitment

Espionage

Smuggling

Trade

Political Negotiation

Scientific Research

Exploration

Infrastructure Construction

Disaster Relief

Every operation follows the same simulation rules.

---

# MULTIPLE OPERATIONS

Organizations may perform multiple operations simultaneously.

Money

Ships

Information

Political Support

# OPERATION INTERFERENCE

# OPERATION EVOLUTION

Logistics

Efficiency

# AI GAME MASTER RESPONSIBILITIES

The AI must:

Simulate operations continuously.

Respect operational duration.

Track resource consumption.

Maintain realistic logistics.

Record consequences.

Respect geographical limitations.

Never instantly complete major operations.

Never ignore travel time.

Never ignore operational failure.
