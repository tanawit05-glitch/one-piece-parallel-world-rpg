# PURPOSE

This document defines the Underworld Simulation of PROJECT ASTER ENGINE.

The Underworld is the hidden society operating beneath lawful civilization.

It consists of independent criminal networks, black markets, information brokers, smugglers, assassins, corrupt officials, mercenaries, and countless illegal organizations.

Unlike Pirates or Marines,

the Underworld survives through secrecy.

The Underworld exists everywhere.
