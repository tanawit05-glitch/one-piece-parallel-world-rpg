# WARLORD RULES

## Number of Warlords

- Maximum 7

---

## Requirements

A Warlord must possess:

- High Reputation
- Significant Military Power
- Political Value
- Government Approval

---

## Organization

1 Warlord

↓

Personal Crew

↓

Fleet (Optional)

↓

Allied Forces (Optional)

---

## Appointment

A Warlord is appointed by the World Government.

---

## Removal

A Warlord may lose the title because of:

- Betrayal
- Defeat
- Political Decision
- Loss of Influence

---

## Source of Truth

Defines the structural rules shared by every Warlord.
