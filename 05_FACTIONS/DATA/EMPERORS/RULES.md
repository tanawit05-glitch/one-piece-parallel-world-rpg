# EMPEROR RULES

## Number of Emperors

- Always 4

---

## Minimum Requirements

An Emperor must possess:

- Massive Influence
- Powerful Crew
- Large Territory
- Fleet
- Political Power

---

## Organization

1 Emperor

↓

3–5 Commanders

↓

Fleet Captains

↓

Crew Members

↓

Allied Crews

---

## Territory

Every Emperor controls:

- Multiple Islands
- Safe Ports
- Supply Routes

---

## Succession

If an Emperor falls:

- Territory becomes unstable.
- Rival factions compete.
- A new Emperor may emerge.

The title is earned, never inherited.

---

## Source of Truth

Defines the structural rules shared by every Emperor.
