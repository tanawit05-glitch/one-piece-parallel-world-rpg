# PURPOSE

This document defines diplomatic interactions between all factions within PROJECT ASTER ENGINE.

Diplomacy determines how organizations cooperate, negotiate, threaten, manipulate, and wage war.

Diplomatic relationships continuously evolve according to world events.

Diplomacy is never static.

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

War

Is

Only

One

Form

Of

Diplomacy.

Every faction pursues its own interests.

Cooperation exists only while it remains beneficial.

---

# CORE PRINCIPLES

Every faction maintains diplomatic relationships with other factions.

Diplomacy influences:

- Trade
- Intelligence
- Military Support
- Territory
- Politics
- Economy
- Operations

Relationships are dynamic.

No alliance lasts forever.

---

# DIPLOMATIC STATUS

Relationships may exist as:

Alliance

Friendly

Cooperative

Neutral

Suspicious

Hostile

Open Conflict

War

Secret Alliance

Proxy Conflict

Status changes gradually unless major events occur.

---

# ALLIANCE

Both factions cooperate.

Possible benefits:

Joint Operations

Shared Intelligence

Military Assistance

Trade

Technology Exchange

Safe Harbors

Alliance requires maintenance.

---

# FRIENDLY

Relations are positive.

Both sides are willing to negotiate.

Limited cooperation occurs naturally.

---

# COOPERATIVE

Organizations work together on specific objectives.

The relationship may be temporary.

Completion of objectives may end cooperation.

---

# NEUTRAL

Neither cooperation nor hostility exists.

Information exchange is limited.

Trade may continue.

---

# SUSPICIOUS

Organizations monitor one another.

Diplomatic tension increases.

Espionage becomes more common.

Military preparation may begin.

---

# HOSTILE

Direct cooperation ends.

Sanctions

Embargoes

Blockades

Political Pressure

Covert Operations

become common.

---

# OPEN CONFLICT

Military operations occur regularly.

Territorial clashes become frequent.

Negotiation remains possible.

---

# WAR

Diplomacy has completely failed.

Organizations seek victory through force.

War affects:

Population

Economy

Territory

Reputation

World Stability

---

# SECRET ALLIANCE

Public enemies

Private allies.

Secret alliances may involve:

Information Sharing

Funding

Hidden Logistics

Political Manipulation

Mercenary Support

Discovery may trigger international consequences.

---

# PROXY CONFLICT

Organizations avoid direct confrontation.

Instead they support:

Mercenaries

Pirates

Rebels

Criminal Groups

Local Governments

Proxy conflicts often last longer than wars.
