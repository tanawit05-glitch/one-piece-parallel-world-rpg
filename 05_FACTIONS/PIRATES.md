# PIRATES

## Purpose

Defines how pirate organizations operate, grow, compete, and survive throughout the world.

---

## Core Mission

- Pursue freedom
- Build reputation
- Acquire wealth
- Protect the crew
- Expand influence
- Survive in the New World

## Responsibilities

- Recruit members
- Explore the sea
- Acquire resources
- Protect the crew
- Develop the fleet
- Establish pirate bases
- Expand influence

---

## Resources

- Ships
- Crew
- Money
- Food
- Weapons
- Medical Supplies
- Information
- Territory

Resources are limited and must be managed.

---

## Operations

Pirates may:

- Raid
- Explore
- Hunt treasure
- Smuggle goods
- Trade
- Recruit
- Repair ships
- Form alliances

Operations consume time and resources.

---

## Territory

Pirate influence may extend over:

- Islands
- Ports
- Hidden Bases
- Trade Routes
- Sea Routes

Territory is never permanent.

---

## Relations

Friendly

- Allied Crews

Hostile

- Marines
- World Government

Variable

- Emperors
- Warlords
- Cross Guild
- Revolutionary Army
- Underworld

Relationships change according to world events.

---

## Growth

Growth depends on:

- Reputation
- Recruitment
- Wealth
- Successful operations
- Territorial expansion

Growth increases both influence and responsibility.

---

## Decline

A pirate crew may decline because of:

- Defeat
- Resource shortages
- Crew losses
- Betrayal
- Marine pressure
- Leadership failure

Decline affects the entire organization.

---

## Simulation Rules

- Operate independently
- Never possess perfect information
- Respect logistics
- Respect travel time
- Adapt to changing conditions
- Pursue opportunities based on risk and reward

---

## AI Rules

Every pirate crew has its own personality, goals, and code.

No pirate organization should behave identically to another.
