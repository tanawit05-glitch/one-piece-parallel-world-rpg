# PURPOSE

The Bounty System represents the World Government's official threat assessment framework.

A bounty is not a measurement of combat strength.

It is an estimation of how dangerous an individual or organization is to the stability of the world.

The Bounty System affects:

- Reputation
- Marine Response
- Government Attention
- Civilian Behavior
- Underworld Interest
- Pirate Recognition
- Political Pressure

The purpose of this system is to ensure that every bounty issued within PROJECT ASTER ENGINE follows consistent, logical, and simulation-driven rules.

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Bounties measure Threat.

Not Power.

Not Level.

Not Experience.

Not Popularity.

A weak revolutionary leader may possess a higher bounty than a powerful pirate if their actions threaten world stability more severely.

Every bounty exists because the World Government has a reason to fear the target.

---

# CORE PRINCIPLES

A bounty is:

- Political
- Military
- Economic
- Symbolic

Every bounty represents the government's judgment.

It does not represent objective truth.

Different factions may evaluate the same individual differently.

Threat Assessment considers multiple factors.

Combat Ability

Military Leadership

Strategic Intelligence

Knowledge Risk

Criminal Activity

Public Influence

Faction Leadership

Ideological Threat

Economic Damage

Civilian Casualties

Government Losses

International Incidents

The AI Game Master evaluates all factors together.

No single factor determines a bounty.

---

# THREAT LEVELS

Threat Levels classify danger before determining bounty values.

## Negligible

Minor nuisance.

No significant attention.

---

## Local Threat

Dangerous within one island or local region.

Marine patrols may respond.

---

## Regional Threat

Capable of destabilizing multiple islands.

Regional Marine Headquarters become involved.

---

## National Threat

Threatens an entire kingdom.

Government agencies become active.

---

## Global Threat

Actions influence multiple seas.

High-ranking Marines begin investigations.

---

## Catastrophic Threat

Capable of altering world balance.

Highest priority target.

False identification

# REPUTATION INTERACTION

Bounty influences Reputation.

Reputation influences public interpretation.

Neither system replaces the other.

A high bounty may create fear.

A high reputation may create admiration.

These outcomes are independent.

---

# RELATIONSHIP INTERACTION

Individual NPCs may ignore official bounties.

Relationship always overrides public opinion for personal decisions.

An NPC who deeply trusts the player may continue helping them despite an enormous bounty.

---

# FALSE BOUNTIES

Government misinformation may occur.

The AI Game Master may use false bounties as narrative events.

Consider political consequences.

Respect world information flow.

Differentiate threat from combat ability.
