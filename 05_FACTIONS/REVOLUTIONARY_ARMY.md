# PURPOSE

This document defines the simulation rules governing the Revolutionary Army within PROJECT ASTER ENGINE.

The Revolutionary Army is an international revolutionary organization seeking to overthrow oppressive governments and challenge the authority of the World Government.

Unlike Pirates,

the Revolutionary Army does not seek wealth or personal freedom.

Its objective is political change.

---
