# KINGDOMS

## Purpose

Defines how kingdoms govern their people, manage their territory, and interact with the world.

---

## Core Mission

- Protect citizens
- Maintain stability
- Develop the economy
- Defend national territory
- Preserve sovereignty

---

## Organization

King / Queen

↓

Royal Family

↓

Government Officials

↓

Military

↓

Local Governors

↓

Citizens

Every kingdom governs independently.

---

## Responsibilities

- Maintain law and order
- Collect taxes
- Manage the economy
- Defend borders
- Build infrastructure
- Support public welfare
- Conduct diplomacy

---

## Resources

- Population
- Treasury
- Military
- Agriculture
- Industry
- Trade
- Natural Resources

Resources determine a kingdom's strength.

---

## Diplomacy

Kingdoms may:

- Form alliances
- Sign trade agreements
- Declare war
- Request Marine support
- Negotiate treaties

Diplomatic relations change over time.

---

## Relations

Friendly

- Allied Kingdoms
- World Government

Hostile

- Invading Forces

Variable

- Marines
- Revolutionary Army
- Pirates
- Cross Guild
- Underworld

Relations depend on current events.

---

## Growth

Growth depends on:

- Population
- Economy
- Technology
- Infrastructure
- Trade
- Political Stability

Growth requires long-term investment.

---

## Decline

A kingdom may decline because of:

- War
- Corruption
- Famine
- Economic Collapse
- Civil Unrest
- Natural Disasters

Decline affects every aspect of the nation.

---

## Simulation Rules

- Govern continuously
- Never possess perfect information
- Respect logistics
- Respect diplomacy
- Adapt to world events
- Balance military and economy

---

## AI Rules

Every kingdom has unique laws, culture, economy, and political goals.

No two kingdoms should behave identically.
