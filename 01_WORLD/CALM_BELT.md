# CALM_BELT

---

# Purpose

`CALM_BELT.md` defines the simulation rules governing the Calm Belt.

The Calm Belt is one of the world's most dangerous maritime regions.

It functions as a natural barrier separating the Four Blues from the Grand Line while creating significant navigational, ecological, and strategic challenges.

This document establishes the environmental rules required for accurate world simulation.

---

# Scope

This document defines:

- Calm Belt Characteristics
- Ocean Behavior
- Navigation Restrictions
- Sea King Habitat
- Environmental Hazards
- Travel Limitations
- Strategic Influence

This document does not define:

- Individual Sea Kings
- Combat Rules
- Ship Systems
- Navigation Equipment
- Weather Mechanics

Those systems are defined in their respective documents.

---

# Core Principle

The Calm Belt is naturally hostile to conventional navigation.

Its danger comes from environmental conditions rather than constant combat.

Crossing the Calm Belt is possible only through exceptional preparation, specialized technology, or extraordinary capability.

---

# Environmental Identity

The Calm Belt is characterized by:

- Almost complete absence of natural wind
- Extremely weak ocean currents
- Unusually calm surface conditions
- Dense Sea King populations
- Limited maritime traffic

These conditions remain persistent throughout the region.

---

# Wind Conditions

Natural wind is nearly nonexistent.

Sailing vessels relying solely on wind propulsion become largely immobile.

Alternative propulsion methods may be required.

Detailed ship mechanics are defined in SHIPS.md.

---

# Ocean Currents

Ocean currents are minimal.

Ships cannot rely on natural current flow for navigation.

Movement becomes significantly slower without active propulsion.

---

# Sea King Habitat

The Calm Belt is the primary habitat of Sea Kings.

Sea King encounters are considered an inherent environmental risk.

Encounter probability depends on factors such as:

- Noise
- Ship size
- Environmental disturbance
- Local ecosystem

Sea King behavior is determined by future creature systems.

---

# Navigation Difficulty

Navigation within the Calm Belt is inherently dangerous.

Potential challenges include:

- Loss of mobility
- Limited escape options
- Reduced maneuverability
- Increased exposure to Sea Kings
- Isolation from trade routes

Navigation systems must respect these limitations.

---

# Travel Risk

Travel through the Calm Belt always involves elevated risk.

Potential outcomes include:

- Delayed travel
- Ship damage
- Resource depletion
- Crew fatigue
- Sea King encounters
- Emergency course changes

Safe passage should never be assumed.

---

# Strategic Importance

Despite its dangers, the Calm Belt possesses significant strategic value.

Possible uses include:

- Concealed travel
- Military operations
- Scientific research
- Rare resource exploration
- Hidden facilities

Strategic activity should remain uncommon due to inherent risk.

---

# Economic Influence

The Calm Belt limits commercial transportation.

Trade routes naturally avoid the region whenever possible.

Crossing costs are significantly higher than conventional routes.

Economic simulations should reflect these logistical challenges.

---

# Ecological Balance

The Calm Belt functions as a unique ecosystem.

Its biological diversity differs significantly from surrounding seas.

Large marine life dominates the region.

Environmental disturbances may temporarily alter local ecological behavior.

---

# Accessibility

The Calm Belt is not completely inaccessible.

However,

successful entry and exit require exceptional circumstances.

The AI Game Master should evaluate access based on:

- Ship capability
- Crew experience
- Navigation systems
- Environmental conditions
- Current world state

---

# World Simulation

The Calm Belt continues evolving independently.

Possible ongoing developments include:

- Sea King migration
- Ecological changes
- Hidden discoveries
- Scientific expeditions
- Military observation
- Illegal operations

Simulation continues regardless of player presence.

---

# AI Game Master Responsibilities

The AI must:

- Preserve environmental tension.
- Respect natural navigation limitations.
- Simulate realistic ecological behavior.
- Maintain consistent Sea King presence.
- Apply identical environmental rules to all characters.

The AI must never:

- Treat the Calm Belt as ordinary ocean.
- Ignore navigation penalties.
- Eliminate environmental danger without justification.
- Allow effortless crossing.
