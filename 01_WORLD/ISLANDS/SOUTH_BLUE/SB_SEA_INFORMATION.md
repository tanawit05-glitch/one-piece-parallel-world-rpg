# SOUTH BLUE SEA INFORMATION
# ==================================================

World Type: One Piece Parallel World

---

# OVERVIEW

South Blue is a volatile and diverse sea compared to East Blue.

While East Blue is stable and beginner-friendly, South Blue is:

- More politically fragmented
- More biologically diverse
- More crime-prone in certain regions
- More extreme in environmental variation

It is considered a “transition sea” between beginner seas and the Grand Line.

---

# SEA CHARACTERISTICS

Navigation Difficulty

Medium

Weather Stability

Medium–Low (unpredictable zones exist)

Sea King Activity

Moderate

Average Wave Height

2–8 meters

Visibility

Variable (fog zones in multiple regions)

---

# REGIONAL TYPES

South Blue is divided into 4 major environmental categories:

## 1. Trade-Heavy Zones
- Sunburst Island
- Aurora Port
- Coralhaven

Stable economy, strong Marine presence.

---

## 2. Lawless Zones
- Red Lagoon
- Blood Mangrove (restricted)

High piracy, smuggling networks.

---

## 3. Natural/Peaceful Zones
- Baterilla
- Torino Kingdom

Low crime, high agriculture and research.

---

## 4. Anomaly Zones
- Sandglass Key
- Windsong Isle (wind distortion fields)

Unstable physics or extreme environmental effects.

---

# NAVIGATION SYSTEM

South Blue requires:

- Standard Compass
- Sea Charts
- Wind Reading (important in Windsong Isle region)
- Coral Depth Mapping (Coralhaven region)

Log Pose is optional but recommended near anomaly zones.

---

# STRATEGIC ROLE

South Blue functions as:

- 🌾 Food supplier (Baterilla, Sunburst)
- 🔬 Research hub (Torino, Sandglass Key)
- ⚓ Trade corridor (Aurora Port, Coralhaven)
- ☠️ Criminal network hub (Red Lagoon)
- 🌪️ Navigation innovation zone (Windsong Isle)

---

# MILITARY IMPORTANCE

Compared to East Blue:

- More Marine specialization units
- More experimental operations
- More classified zones (Sandglass Key, Blood Mangrove)

---

# PIRACY LEVEL

Overall Threat

Medium–High

Notes:
- Fewer “massive pirate crews”
- More organized syndicates in specific zones
- More hidden operations than open warfare

---

# WORLD GOVERNMENT INTEREST

High Priority Zones:

- Sandglass Key (TOP SECRET)
- Blood Mangrove (BIOHAZARD CLASSIFIED)
- Red Lagoon (CRIMINAL NETWORK MONITORING)
- Aurora Port (STRATEGIC NAVIGATION HUB)

---

# GM NOTES

South Blue is designed for:

- Mid-campaign progression
- Transition from beginner to advanced world understanding
- Introduction of hidden world mechanics (time, biology, wind, smuggling networks)

Player experience should shift from:
“exploration” → “systems awareness of the world”
