# OVERVIEW

Windsong Isle is a fast-moving trade and travel hub known for its constant strong winds that circle the island year-round. These winds allow ships to accelerate unusually fast, making the island a critical “speed corridor” for South Blue navigation.

It is also famous for its musical culture, where wind patterns are believed to carry “songs of the sea,” inspiring musicians, navigators, and pirates alike.
