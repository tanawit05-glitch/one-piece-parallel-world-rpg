# OVERVIEW

Sunburst Island is a prosperous tropical island famous for its nearly year-round sunshine, fertile volcanic soil, and vast citrus plantations. Nicknamed "The Island of Endless Summer," it supplies fruit, sugar, and tropical products throughout South Blue.

Although peaceful on the surface, competition between merchant companies and fruit exporters creates constant political tension beneath everyday life.
