# ==================================================
# SOUTH BLUE ECONOMY
# ==================================================

World Type: One Piece Parallel World

---

# OVERVIEW

South Blue Economy is highly fragmented, with strong specialization by island type.

Unlike East Blue which is stable and uniform, South Blue operates as a “cluster economy system”:

- Each island type controls one major resource
- Trade routes are more dangerous but more profitable
- Black markets are more organized and region-based

---

# MAIN ECONOMIC STRUCTURE

## 1. Agricultural Core
(Baterilla, Sunburst Island)

- Food production
- Livestock
- Fruit exports
- Grain supply chains

---

## 2. Maritime Trade Core
(Aurora Port, Coralhaven)

- Shipping logistics
- Navigation services
- Marine infrastructure
- Pearl / mineral trade

---

## 3. Research & Knowledge Core
(Torino Kingdom, Sandglass Key)

- Medicine
- Botany
- Navigation science
- Temporal research (classified)

---

## 4. Shadow Economy Core
(Red Lagoon, Blood Mangrove)

- Smuggling
- Illegal weapons
- Biological trade
- Black market auctions

---

# CURRENCY

Standard Currency

- Belly (Universal)

Secondary Systems

- Trade Credits (Merchant Guild)
- Black Tokens (Illegal market only)

---

# MAJOR EXPORTS

- Fish & seafood
- Citrus & tropical fruit
- Navigation equipment
- Medical herbs
- Pearls & coral materials
- Illegal artifacts (unofficial)

---

# MAJOR IMPORTS

- Steel & weapons
- Medicine
- Luxury goods
- Ship components
- Scientific equipment

---

# TRADE DYNAMICS

- Aurora Port = Main trade hub
- Coralhaven = Underwater trade routes
- Red Lagoon = Illegal redistribution center
- Windsong Isle = High-speed logistics corridor

---

# ECONOMIC RISKS

- Pirate interception
- Syndicate monopolies
- Environmental instability zones
- Government restriction zones (Sandglass Key, Blood Mangrove)

---

# GM NOTES

South Blue economy is designed to create:

- Conflict between legal and illegal trade
- Player-driven supply chain decisions
- Regional economic identity per island cluster
