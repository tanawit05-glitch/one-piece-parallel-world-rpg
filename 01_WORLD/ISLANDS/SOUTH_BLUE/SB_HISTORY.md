# ==================================================
# SOUTH BLUE HISTORY
# ==================================================

World Type: One Piece Parallel World

---

# OVERVIEW

South Blue history is defined by fragmentation, independent island development, and limited centralized control compared to other seas.

Unlike the Grand Line, South Blue never experienced a single world-shaping war—but instead evolved through **localized crises, trade expansion, and hidden government experiments**.

---

# 1. 🌱 EARLY ERA (FOUNDATION AGE)

- Islands developed independently
- Fishing and agriculture were dominant
- Navigation was primitive and coastal-only

Key Event:
- Formation of early island settlements like Baterilla and Torino Kingdom

Result:
👉 Stable but isolated civilizations

---

# 2. ⚓ AGE OF TRADE EXPANSION

- Merchant guilds begin connecting islands
- First structured sea routes established
- Early Marine presence introduced

Key Event:
- Creation of Aurora Port as a central trade hub

Result:
👉 Economic integration begins across South Blue

---

# 3. ☠️ AGE OF PIRATE FRAGMENTATION

- Pirate crews emerge across trade routes
- No large pirate empires form
- Syndicates begin replacing traditional piracy in some regions

Key Event:
- Rise of Red Lagoon black market network

Result:
👉 Crime becomes system-based instead of crew-based

---

# 4. 🧬 AGE OF SECRET EXPERIMENTATION

(World Government classified era)

- Biological research begins in hidden zones
- Environmental experiments conducted
- Early anomaly zones emerge

Key Events:
- Blood Mangrove origin incident
- Sandglass Key temporal distortion event
- Coralhaven underwater expansion tests

Result:
👉 Creation of “forbidden islands”

---

# 5. 🌪️ MODERN ERA (CURRENT TIMELINE)

South Blue becomes:

- A multi-layer economic sea
- A navigation research hub
- A controlled crime ecosystem
- A testing ground for classified World Government projects

---

# MAJOR HISTORICAL INCIDENTS

## 🔴 Red Lagoon Formation Event
- Collapse of old port authority
- Syndicates took control
- Official government withdrawal

---

## 🧬 Blood Mangrove Incident
- Biological contamination outbreak
- Entire research base lost
- Area declared quarantine zone

---

## 🕰️ Sandglass Key Anomaly
- Temporal distortion first recorded
- Multiple expedition disappearances
- Area sealed by Chrono Division

---

## ⚡ Windsong Navigation Revolution
- Discovery of wind-speed travel corridors
- Major increase in trade efficiency
- Pirate mobility drastically increased

---

# WORLD GOVERNMENT ROLE

South Blue was not directly conquered or unified.

Instead, the government operates through:

- Trade regulation hubs
- Marine specialized divisions
- Classified research zones
- Indirect syndicate monitoring

---

# PIRACY EVOLUTION

1. Early Era → Small pirate crews
2. Trade Era → Route-based piracy
3. Modern Era → Syndicate piracy + anomaly exploitation

---

# CURRENT STATE OF SOUTH BLUE

South Blue is considered:

- Stable economically
- Unstable in hidden zones
- Highly valuable strategically
- Dangerous in uncharted regions

---

# GM NOTES

South Blue history is designed to:

- Explain why each island is unique
- Justify anomalies (Blood Mangrove / Sandglass Key)
- Support faction-based storytelling
- Provide long-term campaign evolution hooks

Core idea:
👉 “History is layered, not linear”
