# ==================================================
# SOUTH BLUE BOUNTIES SYSTEM
# ==================================================

World Type: One Piece Parallel World

---

# OVERVIEW

Bounty systems in South Blue are more complex than East Blue due to:

- Higher presence of syndicates
- More classified threats
- Environmental hazards affecting “danger scoring”
- Marine division specialization per region

Bounties are not only based on combat power but also:

- Economic disruption
- Information sensitivity
- Environmental danger
- Political influence

---

# BASE BOUNTY SCALE (SOUTH BLUE)

| Threat Level | Description | Bounty Range |
|---|---|---:|
| Low Threat | Bandits / small crews | 2M – 8M |
| Rookie Pirates | Early-stage crews | 5M – 15M |
| Established Crews | Regional pirates | 15M – 40M |
| High Threat | Syndicate-level crews | 40M – 120M |
| Disaster Class | Island destabilizers | 120M – 300M |
| Classified Threat | World Government interest | 300M+ (hidden) |

---

# REGION MODIFIERS

## 🌾 Peaceful Zones (Baterilla / Torino)
- -30% bounty reduction
- Crime visibility is low
- Marines prioritize capture over kill

---

## ⚓ Trade Zones (Aurora / Coralhaven / Sunburst)
- Standard bounty
- Higher economic damage weight

---

## ☠️ Lawless Zones (Red Lagoon)
- +50% bounty multiplier
- Syndicate-affiliated crimes included

---

## 🧬 Anomaly Zones (Sandglass Key / Blood Mangrove)
- Bounties may be **classified**
- Some targets have “UNKNOWN VALUE”
- Marine may suppress bounty publication

---

# SPECIAL CLASSIFICATIONS

## 🔴 “BLACK FILE” TARGETS
- No public bounty
- Only Marine HQ + Government records

Examples:
- Sandglass Key anomalies
- Rotmother-type entities
- Chrono research incidents

---

## ☣️ BIOHAZARD TARGETS
(Blood Mangrove category)

- Living mutation threats
- Contagion risk
- Environmental corruption entities

---

## ⚡ FAST ROUTE PIRATES
(Windsong Isle region)

- Bounty adjusted based on ship speed capability
- Pursuit difficulty increases value

---

# DEVIL FRUIT MODIFIER

If user has Devil Fruit:

- Paramecia → +20–50%
- Zoan → +30–70%
- Logia → +100%+

Special Fruits:
- Time / Biological / Wind-related → Classified scaling

---

# MARINE RESPONSE FACTOR

Bounty increases if:

- Marine casualties occur
- Government property destroyed
- Trade routes disrupted
- Classified zones accessed

---

# GM NOTES

This system is designed to:

- Make each island feel economically different
- Link bounty with world systems (not just fighting)
- Allow “story crimes” (information theft, exploration) to matter
