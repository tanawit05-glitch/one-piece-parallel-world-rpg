# OVERVIEW

Blood Mangrove is a forbidden swamp island covered entirely by massive crimson mangrove forests that bleed red resin when cut.

The island is feared across South Blue due to its toxic environment, extreme disease outbreaks, and the presence of mutated wildlife.

It is officially marked as **“No-Entry Zone”** by multiple Marine divisions.

Rumors claim the island was once used for illegal biological experiments during early World Government expansion.
