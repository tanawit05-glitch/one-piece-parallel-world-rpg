# OVERVIEW

Aurora Port is a northern maritime hub famous for its rare phenomenon: glowing sea fog and bioluminescent waters that illuminate the entire coastline at night.

It is one of South Blue’s most important cold-water trade ports, connecting southern routes with northern shipping lanes and acting as a key transfer point for navigation toward the Grand Line approach routes.

The island is known for its disciplined naval presence, advanced navigation science, and strict trade regulation.
