# OVERVIEW

Torino Kingdom is a remote island located deep within South Blue, famous for its enormous forests, towering trees, unusual wildlife, and the harmonious relationship between its inhabitants and nature.

Unlike most kingdoms, Torino has never pursued military expansion or commercial dominance. Its greatest treasures are knowledge of medicine, rare plants, and ecological preservation.

In the Parallel World, Torino has become one of the world's leading centers for botanical research. Doctors, herbalists, scholars, and explorers travel here to study plants found nowhere else in the world.
