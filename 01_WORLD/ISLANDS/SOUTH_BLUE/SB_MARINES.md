# SOUTH BLUE MARINES SYSTEM
# ==================================================

World Type: One Piece Parallel World

---

# OVERVIEW

The South Blue Marine structure is more fragmented and specialized than East Blue.

Instead of a single unified force, it operates as a **multi-division adaptive system**, designed to handle:

- Syndicate crime networks
- Environmental anomaly zones
- High-speed piracy routes
- Biological hazard regions
- Temporal instability areas

---

# MAIN STRUCTURE

## 1. SOUTH BLUE HEADQUARTERS
Central command overseeing all operations.

Responsibilities:
- Strategic planning
- Bounty classification approval
- Classified zone authorization

---

## 2. SPECIALIZED DIVISIONS

### ⚓ Trade Protection Fleet
(Sea routes: Aurora / Coralhaven / Sunburst)

- Escort merchant ships
- Protect trade corridors
- Anti-piracy interception

---

### ⚡ FAST RESPONSE UNIT
(Windsong Isle influence)

- High-speed pursuit ships
- Interception of fast-route pirates
- Rapid deployment across islands

---

### 🧬 BIOHAZARD CONTAINMENT UNIT
(Blood Mangrove)

- Quarantine enforcement
- Mutation containment
- Experimental sample retrieval

---

### 🕰️ CHRONOLOGY DIVISION (CLASSIFIED)
(Sandglass Key)

- Temporal anomaly research
- Time distortion containment
- Expedition suppression

---

### ☠️ UNDERCOVER INTELLIGENCE UNIT
(Red Lagoon)

- Syndicate infiltration

---

# CORRUPTION LEVEL

South Blue Marines are:

- Low corruption in peaceful zones
- Moderate corruption in trade hubs
- High risk infiltration in Red Lagoon border regions

Corruption is treated as:
👉 “Story event generator”, not default behavior

---

# RELATIONSHIP NETWORK

## Civilians

Strong cooperation

# GM NOTES

South Blue Marines are designed to:

- Feel like multiple organizations, not one army
- React dynamically to island types
- Create layered conflict (law vs anomaly vs economy)

Key design principle:
👉 “Different justice for different reality zones”
