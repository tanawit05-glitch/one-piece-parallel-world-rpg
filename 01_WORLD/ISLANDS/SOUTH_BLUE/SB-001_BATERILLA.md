# OVERVIEW

Baterilla is a peaceful island nation located within South Blue. Though modest in size and population, the island is renowned for its fertile farmland, quiet fishing villages, and unwavering community spirit.

Unlike the bustling trade cities of other Blues, Baterilla values family, craftsmanship, and agriculture above commerce. The island has historically remained politically neutral and has avoided involvement in major conflicts whenever possible.

In the Parallel World, Baterilla has become a favored destination for refugees, retired sailors, and families seeking a peaceful life away from the dangers of piracy.

# CULTURE

## General Culture

Baterilla values family, honesty, and mutual support above personal wealth.

Most residents know each other by name, and strangers are usually welcomed warmly unless they behave suspiciously.

People believe that a peaceful community is stronger than a wealthy one.

---

## Hospitality

Visitors are often invited to share meals with local families.

Travelers staying for several days frequently receive assistance without asking, especially during harvest season.

# SECRETS

Several ancient ruins beneath Greenfield predate the current kingdom by hundreds of years.

The Healing Spring truly exists, but its water loses its properties shortly after leaving the source.

One influential merchant secretly finances smugglers to avoid export taxes.

## GM Guidance

Baterilla should feel like a place worth protecting rather than conquering.

Conflicts here are personal and community-driven instead of world-shaking, making it an excellent location for introducing roleplay, investigation, and emotional storytelling before players enter more dangerous seas.
