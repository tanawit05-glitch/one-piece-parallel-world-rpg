# OVERVIEW

Sandglass Key is a legendary and heavily restricted island located at the edge of South Blue’s mapped territory.

It is known for its abnormal time-related phenomena, shifting landscapes, and ancient ruins that appear to predate recorded history of the seas.

Most navigation charts label this island as:

> “DO NOT APPROACH – STATIC SPACE ANOMALY”

Only a few Marine expedition logs confirm its existence.
