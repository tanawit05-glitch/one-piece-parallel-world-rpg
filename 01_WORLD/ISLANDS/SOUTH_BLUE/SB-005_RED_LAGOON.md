# OVERVIEW

Red Lagoon is a lawless island formed around a massive blood-red saltwater lagoon surrounded by volcanic cliffs. It is infamous as a haven for pirates, smugglers, bounty hunters, and underground brokers.

Unlike most islands, Red Lagoon has no central government. Instead, it is controlled indirectly by competing criminal syndicates that maintain a fragile balance of power.

The island is often referred to as **"South Blue’s Shadow Market"**.
