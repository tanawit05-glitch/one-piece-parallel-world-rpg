# OVERVIEW

Coralhaven is an island built around massive coral reef systems that extend both above and below sea level. The island is famous for its marine biodiversity, underwater settlements, and advanced diving culture.

It is one of the few islands in South Blue where humans, Fish-Men, and other sea-adapted species coexist closely due to the island’s unique semi-aquatic environment.

Coralhaven culture is deeply connected to the sea.

Children are trained to swim before they can fully read, and diving is considered a rite of passage.

Surface dwellers and underwater residents have distinct but cooperative traditions.
