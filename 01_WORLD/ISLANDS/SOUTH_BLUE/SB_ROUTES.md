# SOUTH BLUE ROUTES SYSTEM
# ==================================================

World Type: One Piece Parallel World

---

# OVERVIEW

South Blue trade routes are significantly more complex than East Blue due to:

- Environmental anomaly zones
- Syndicate-controlled corridors
- High-speed wind routes
- Underwater passage networks
- Classified navigation paths

Routes are divided into **5 major categories**.

---

# 1. 🌾 STANDARD TRADE ROUTE NETWORK

Main civilian and merchant routes.

## Primary Chain Route
Baterilla → Sunburst Island → Aurora Port → Coralhaven → Loguetown Gateway (East Blue connection point)

Purpose:
- Food trade
- Merchant transport
- Civilian travel

---

# 2. ☠️ DANGEROUS ROUTES

High piracy / conflict zones

## Red Corridor Route
Sunburst Island → Red Lagoon → Coralhaven

Risks:
- Syndicate interception
- Illegal taxation
- Ship hijacking

---

## Mangrove Shadow Route
Coralhaven → Blood Mangrove → Unmapped exit points

Risks:
- Biological contamination
- Navigation loss
- Ship destruction

---

# 3. ⚡ FAST WIND ROUTES

(Windsong Isle system)

## Aeolus Speed Corridor
Windsong Isle → Aurora Port → Coralhaven (High-speed loop)

Advantages:
- 2–3x faster travel
- Reduced fuel/time cost

Risks:
- Sudden wind storms
- Ship instability zones

---

# 4. 🧭 RESEARCH / RESTRICTED ROUTES

## Northern Knowledge Path
Torino Kingdom → Sandglass Key → Aurora Port (classified)

Purpose:
- Research expeditions
- Government operations
- Artifact recovery missions

Status:
- Restricted access
- Marine escort required

---

# 5. 🌊 ANOMALY ROUTES (UNKNOWN NAVIGATION)

## Temporal Drift Route
Sandglass Key → Unknown exit points

Effects:
- Time distortion
- Route shifting
- Possible re-entry in different coordinates

---

## Coral Depth Network
Coralhaven → Underwater tunnel systems → unknown sea exits

Effects:
- Submarine navigation required
- Risk of entrapment
- Hidden smuggling access

---

# WORLD GOVERNMENT CONTROL POINTS

- Aurora Port (inspection hub)
- Sandglass Key (black classified zone)
- Blood Mangrove perimeter blockade
- Red Lagoon surveillance border

---

# ROUTE DANGER SCALE

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 1 | Safe trade route |
| 2 | Minor pirate activity |
| 3 | Active danger zone |
| 4 | High risk survival route |
| 5 | Extreme / classified anomaly |

---

# GM NOTES

South Blue routes are designed to:

- Connect story arcs between islands naturally
- Force player decision-making in navigation
- Create “risk vs reward travel planning”
- Enable hidden world discovery through anomaly routes

Core idea:
👉 “Travel is gameplay, not just transition”
