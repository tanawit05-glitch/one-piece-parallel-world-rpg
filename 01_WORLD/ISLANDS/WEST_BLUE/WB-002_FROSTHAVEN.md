# FROSTHAVEN

Sea: West Blue  
Classification: Kingdom / Frozen Archipelago  
World Type: One Piece Parallel World  

---

# OVERVIEW

Frosthaven is a massive frozen kingdom located in the far northern reaches of West Blue. Unlike typical winter islands, Frosthaven is permanently covered in ice due to an abnormal oceanic cold current known as the **“White Undertow”**.

The kingdom is both a strategic naval chokepoint and a dangerous scientific anomaly. Ships that pass too close often report navigation failure, frozen compasses, and hallucinations caused by extreme cold magnetic distortion.

Frosthaven is officially a kingdom under World Government oversight, but in reality it is a **semi-autonomous cold-zone fortress civilization**.

---

# ISLAND STRUCTURE

Frosthaven is not a single island but a **cluster of 5 major frozen landmasses**:

- North Spire Island (Capital)
- Iceveil Island (Industrial Zone)
- Glacier Chain (Mining Region)
- Silent Sea Platform (Naval Zone)
- Whitegrave Island (Unknown Restricted Zone)
