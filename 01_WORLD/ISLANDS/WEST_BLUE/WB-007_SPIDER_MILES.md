# 🧭 OVERVIEW

Spider Miles is not a conventional island.

It is a **distributed underground intelligence network** spanning multiple hidden nodes across West Blue, all connected through physical tunnels, coded messengers, and corrupted communication lines.

People say:

> “You never arrive at Spider Miles. You get noticed by it.”

---

Spider Miles is divided into **7 hidden nodes**, each called a “Leg”:

## 🕷️ LEG 1 — Harbor Entry Node
- Fake merchant docks
- Information filtering gate
- Identity verification traps

---

## 🕷️ LEG 2 — Market Echo Node
- Black rumor trading zone
- Fake news distribution center

---

## 🕷️ LEG 3 — Signal Relay Node

- Intercepts World Government transmissions

---

## 🕷️ LEG 4 — Memory Vault Node
- Stores blackmail data
- Erased identities archive
- Political leverage database

---

## 🕷️ LEG 5 — Contract Node
- Assassin contract distribution
- Bounty manipulation center
- Mercenary coordination hub

---

## 🕷️ LEG 6 — Deep Wire Node
- Underground tunnel network

- Emergency escape routes for agents

---

## 🕷️ LEG 7 — CORE SPIDER NODE
