# 🌙 OVERVIEW

Crescent Isle is a semi-mysterious crescent-shaped island known for its **illegal knowledge trade, forbidden artifacts, and information brokers**.

Unlike Thrill Port (chaos economy), Crescent Isle is a **silent economy of secrets**.

People do not come here to buy goods.

They come to buy **truths**.

---

# 🗺️ GEOGRAPHY

Island Shape:
- Crescent moon shaped landmass

Environment:
- Dense fog coastlines
- Floating cliff villages
- Submerged ruins along outer rim

Climate:
- Permanent twilight haze
- Low visibility skies
- Magnetic interference zones

---

# 🏙️ KEY AREAS

## 📚 1. Library of Sighs
- Massive underground archive
- Stores forbidden history
- Books written on unknown materials (stone, metal, bone)

---

## 🕯️ 2. Whisper Market
- Information exchange bazaar
- No money visible exchange (only codes, favors, memories)
- Identity masking field active

---

## 🪶 3. Fogspire District
- Residential + broker housing
- Houses built vertically into cliffs
- Controlled by unseen council

---

## ⚰️ 4. Drowned Archive (Restricted)
- Submerged ruins
- Records of erased nations
- Access requires “Memory Key”

---

# ⚖️ GOVERNMENT

Officially:
- No government

Practically:
- Controlled by “Curators Circle”

Curators Circle:
- 7 anonymous information lords
- Each controls a category of knowledge

---

# 💰 ECONOMY

Primary Currency:
- Memory fragments (converted into data chips or contracts)

Secondary:
- Belly (rare)
- Information debts

Trade Goods:
- Maps
- Historical truth fragments
- Identity records
- Lost technology schematics

---

# 🧠 CORE MECHANIC OF ISLAND

> “Knowledge has weight. And it must be paid for.”

Buying information often requires:
- A memory deletion
- A future favor
- Or surrendering personal history

---
