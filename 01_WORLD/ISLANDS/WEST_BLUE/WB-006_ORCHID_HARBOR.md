# 🌊 OVERVIEW

Orchid Harbor is a lush coastal trading port famous for its **rare medicinal plants, advanced healing techniques, and pharmaceutical exports**.

Unlike Kano (war) or Thrill Port (crime), Orchid Harbor represents:

> “Life preservation through nature and science.”

It is the **primary medical supply hub of West Blue**.
