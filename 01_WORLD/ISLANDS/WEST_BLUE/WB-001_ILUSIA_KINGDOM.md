# ILUSIA KINGDOM

Sea: West Blue  
Classification: Kingdom Island  
World Type: One Piece Parallel World  

---

# OVERVIEW

Ilusia Kingdom is one of the most politically influential nations in West Blue. It is known for its strict governance, advanced bureaucracy, and strong ties with the World Government.

Unlike most kingdoms that rely heavily on military force or trade dominance, Ilusia maintains power through **administrative control, intelligence networks, and economic leverage**.

The kingdom is often described as “quietly absolute” — it does not expand aggressively, but its influence spreads through diplomacy, contracts, and covert agreements.
