# 🧭 OVERVIEW

Thrill Port is a chaotic harbor city built on the edge of legality and survival. Unlike official kingdoms or structured trade hubs, this island thrives on **risk economy**—gambling, bounty trade, illegal recruitment, and underground shipping.

It is known as:

> “The City That Never Sleeps… Because It’s Always Losing Something.”

Thrill Port is a major **neutral ground** where pirates, bounty hunters, mercenaries, and corrupt officials all coexist under fragile balance.

---

# 🌆 CITY STRUCTURE

The island is divided into 4 unstable sectors:

## 🎰 1. Neon Dock District
- Casinos on floating piers
- Illegal betting on pirate battles
- Black market currency exchange

---

## ⚓ 2. Rust Harbor
- Broken ship graveyards
- Pirate repair stations
- Smuggler entry routes

---

## 🕳️ 3. Underdeck City
- Underground tunnels
- Slave trading remnants (officially abolished, still exists underground)
- Hidden bounty broker network

---

## 🎭 4. Velvet Ring
- Elite entertainment zone
- Noble corruption meetings
- Secret assassination contracts

---
