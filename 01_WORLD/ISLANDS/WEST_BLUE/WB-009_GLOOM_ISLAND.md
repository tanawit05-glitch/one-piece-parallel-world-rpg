# 🌫️ WB-009_GLOOM_ISLAND.md

Sea: West Blue  
Classification: Abandoned / Psychological Hazard Island  
World Type: One Piece Parallel World  

---

# 🧭 OVERVIEW

Gloom Island is a half-collapsed island shrouded in permanent darkness and psychological distortion fields.

Unlike other islands in West Blue, Gloom Island is not valuable for trade, war, or resources.

It is valuable because:

> “It remembers things people try to forget.”

---

# 🌑 GEOGRAPHY

Island Type:
- Fragmented landmass

Terrain:
- 50% ruined city structures
- 20% black forests
- 15% underground ruins
- 15% unstable cliff zones

Climate:
- Permanent twilight
- Heavy emotional distortion fog
- Sound dampening atmosphere

---

# 🏚️ KEY REGIONS

## 🕯️ 1. Hollow City Ruins
Collapsed urban zone

- Empty streets with intact buildings
- Echoing footsteps with no source
- Non-linear navigation loops

---

## 🌫️ 2. Memory Fog Forest
Dense black forest

- Causes hallucinations of past memories
- Time perception instability
- Lost travelers reappear as echoes

---

## 🪦 3. Broken Chapel District
Religious ruins

- Unknown religion structures
- Crying sound phenomena
- Ritual markings that rewrite themselves

---

## 🕳️ 4. Underveil Depths
Subterranean tunnels

- Gravity distortion zones
- Missing expedition records
- Unknown entity sightings
