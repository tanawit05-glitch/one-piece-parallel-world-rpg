# 🧭 OVERVIEW

Redstone Cay is a rugged volcanic-mineral island known for its extremely dense underground ore veins and industrial-scale extraction operations.

It is the **primary raw-material powerhouse of West Blue**, supplying metals and minerals to kingdoms, weapon factories, and shipyards across the sea.

People say:

> “If something in West Blue is built strong, it likely came from Redstone Cay.”
