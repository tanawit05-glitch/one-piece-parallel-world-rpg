# 🐉 KANO COUNTRY

Sea: West Blue  
Classification: Militarized Kingdom  
World Type: One Piece Parallel World  

---

# 🌍 OVERVIEW

Kano Country is a heavily militarized kingdom in West Blue known for producing elite soldiers and private mercenary units.

Unlike Ilusia (political control) and Frosthaven (scientific control), Kano is built on:

> “Force is law. Training is survival.”

It functions as a **war economy nation**, supplying soldiers, weapons, and mercenaries across West Blue.
