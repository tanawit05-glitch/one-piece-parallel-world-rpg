# OVERVIEW

Elbaf is the legendary homeland of the Giants, known as the strongest warrior race in the world. It is a mountainous island of colossal scale, where everything—from trees to weapons to cities—is built for beings many times larger than humans.

Elbaf is governed by an ancient warrior culture that values strength, honor, and battlefield legacy above all else. Outsiders are rarely welcomed, and survival on the island requires immense physical and mental endurance.
