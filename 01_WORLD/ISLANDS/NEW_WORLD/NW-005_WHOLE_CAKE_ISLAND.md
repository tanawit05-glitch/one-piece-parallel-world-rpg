# OVERVIEW

Whole Cake Island is the central island of the Totland Archipelago, ruled under the Yonko-level power structure of Big Mom’s territory.

It is a “living political ecosystem” where every resident exists under a system of enforced emotional taxation known as **Soul-Based Governance**.

The island is built as both a utopian food paradise and a psychological control system, where happiness is mandatory and fear is hidden beneath constant celebration.
