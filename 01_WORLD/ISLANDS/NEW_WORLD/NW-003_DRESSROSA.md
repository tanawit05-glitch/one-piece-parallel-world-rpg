# OVERVIEW

Dressrosa is a large, culturally rich kingdom in the New World known for its vibrant cities, gladiator arenas, and heavily controlled political structure.

While appearing as a land of happiness and celebration, the kingdom is built upon layers of manipulation, hidden power control, and suppressed truths beneath its colorful surface.

The island is ruled by a monarchy under World Government recognition, but internal control is heavily influenced by elite factions and hidden power networks.
