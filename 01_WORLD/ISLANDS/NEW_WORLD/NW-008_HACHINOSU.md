# OVERVIEW

Hachinosu is a lawless island in the New World functioning as the central hub for pirate operations, black markets, bounty exchanges, and criminal alliances.

Unlike structured kingdoms, Hachinosu has no official government. Instead, it is governed by pirate syndicates, warlord-level criminals, and shifting power blocs.

It is often referred to as the **“Pirate Capital of the New World”**, where strength defines authority and morality has no legal meaning.
