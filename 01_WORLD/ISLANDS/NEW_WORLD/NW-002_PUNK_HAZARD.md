# OVERVIEW

Punk Hazard is a forbidden island once used as a top-secret research facility by the World Government. After a catastrophic experiment involving extreme environmental manipulation, the island became permanently split into two opposing climates:

- One half frozen in eternal ice ❄️  
- One half engulfed in endless volcanic fire 🔥  

The island is officially abandoned, but remains extremely dangerous due to lingering experiments, toxic environments, and unstable scientific phenomena.
