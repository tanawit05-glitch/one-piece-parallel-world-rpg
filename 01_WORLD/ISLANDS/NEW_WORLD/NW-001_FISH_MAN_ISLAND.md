# OVERVIEW

Fish-Man Island is a massive underwater kingdom located 10,000 meters beneath the Red Line inside a giant bubble dome system.

It is the primary gateway between Paradise and the New World via underwater routes.

The island is inhabited mainly by Fish-Men and Merfolk, forming one of the most politically sensitive civilizations in the world due to historical oppression and conflict with surface humans.

---

# GEOGRAPHY

Island Type: Submarine Dome System

Main Structure:
- Outer Coral Bubble Shield
- Sea Forest Zone
- Ryugu Royal Capital Dome
- Deep Sea Border (Sea King Zone)

Diameter: ~120 km (bubble span)
Depth: 10,000 meters below sea level

# EVENT SYSTEM (DYNAMIC WORLD STATE)

## Tension Level Tracker

Range: 0–100

- 0–20: Peaceful
- 21–40: Stable tension
- 41–60: Political instability
- 61–80: Civil unrest risk
- 81–100: Crisis / lockdown state

---

## Tension Triggers

+5 → Minor crime incident  
+10 → Pirate infiltration detected  
+15 → Slave trade case exposed  
+20 → Royal political conflict  
+25 → Sea King abnormal movement  

---

## Tension Effects

### Low (0–20)
- Normal trade
- Full access to all zones
- NPCs cooperative

### Medium (21–60)
- Increased security checkpoints
- Restricted movement in royal zones
- More Marine involvement

### High (61–80)
- Partial lockdown of Coral Market
- Royal Guard active patrol escalation
- Black market becomes aggressive

### Crisis (81–100)
- Full dome security lockdown
- Sea King evacuation protocol
- World Government intervention risk
