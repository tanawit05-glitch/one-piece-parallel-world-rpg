# OVERVIEW

Wano Country is a heavily isolated nation in the New World, sealed off from the outside world by massive natural barriers and strict national policies.

It is a samurai-based feudal kingdom governed under a Shogunate system. The country is rich in unique resources, especially **Kairoseki (Seastone)** and high-grade weapon forging technology.

Due to its isolation, Wano has developed independently, preserving ancient traditions, martial arts systems, and strict hierarchical social structures.

# SAMURAI COMBAT SYSTEM (CORE MECHANIC)

## BUSHIDO DISCIPLINE SYSTEM

Samurai combat is governed by “discipline states” that affect performance.

---

## DISCIPLINE STATES

### 1. Calm Mind State
- Precision + stability increases
- Critical hit chance boosted
- Defense timing improved

---

### 2. Honor State
- Damage increases when protecting allies
- Buffs triggered by duels or formal challenges

---

### 3. Rage Break State
- Attack power drastically increases
- Defense becomes unstable
- Risk of self-exhaustion

---

### 4. Broken Blade State
- Samurai loses discipline control
- Randomized combat behavior
- High damage output but unstable accuracy

---

# KAIROSEKI SYSTEM (SEASTONE MECHANIC)

## CORE PROPERTY

Kairoseki emits energy that weakens Devil Fruit abilities and disrupts supernatural energy flow.
