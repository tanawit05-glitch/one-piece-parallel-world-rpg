# OVERVIEW

Zou is a massive living island carried on the back of an ancient giant elephant known as **Zunesha**. The island does not remain in a fixed location and continuously moves across the New World sea routes.

Zou is home to the Mink Tribe, a highly advanced humanoid species capable of channeling electrical energy known as **Electro**.

Unlike traditional kingdoms, Zou is not built for trade or expansion. It is a sacred sanctuary, hidden from the world and protected by both nature and isolation.

# ELECTRO SYSTEM (MINK COMBAT CORE)

## CORE MECHANIC: “ELECTRO CHARGE”

Mink warriors generate electrical energy through emotional stimulation and muscle acceleration.

Charge Levels:
- Level 1: Passive static enhancement
- Level 2: Muscle-overcharge speed boost
- Level 3: Weaponized electric discharge
- Level 4: Full-body lightning aura state

---

## ELECTRO COMBAT RULES

- Speed-based scaling increases drastically
- Melee attacks gain shock effect
- Water-based environments increase risk but also output power
- Emotional spikes can trigger sudden power bursts
