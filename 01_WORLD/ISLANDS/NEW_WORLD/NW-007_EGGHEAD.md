# OVERVIEW

Egghead Island is a highly advanced scientific facility operated under World Government research authority. It is known as the “Island of the Future” due to technology that far surpasses current global standards.

The island integrates advanced robotics, artificial intelligence systems, genetic research, and energy manipulation technologies.

Despite its scientific progress, Egghead is also a highly restricted zone where experimental ethics are bypassed for the sake of innovation.
