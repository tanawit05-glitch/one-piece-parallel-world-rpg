# 1. WORLD ECONOMY SYSTEM

## CORE MODEL: “MULTI-POWER CIRCULATION ECONOMY”

The New World economy is not free-market.  
It is controlled by **power blocs + territory control + maritime routes**.

---

## ECONOMIC CURRENCIES

### 1. BELLY (Standard Currency)
- Used in civilian markets
- Weak influence in New World politics

---

### 2. BOUNTY VALUE (INFLUENCE CURRENCY)
- Represents global threat level
- Can be “traded” by bounty hunters / pirates
- Impacts reputation and alliances

---

### 3. BLACK MARKET CREDITS
- Illegal currency
- Used in Hachinosu / underground networks
- Not traceable by World Government systems

---

### 4. STRATEGIC RESOURCES (NON-MONEY VALUE)
- Seastone (Kairoseki)
- Devil Fruits
- Ancient Weapons data
- Log Poses / Eternal Logs
- Weapon-grade metals

---

## ECONOMIC FLOW RULES

- Trade routes = controlled by military/pirate dominance
- Supply chains = vulnerable to war interruption
- Prices = fluctuate based on island stability index

---

## ECONOMIC STABILITY INDEX (0–100)

0–30 → Stable trade  
31–60 → Regional disruption  
61–80 → War economy  
81–100 → Collapse / black market dominance
