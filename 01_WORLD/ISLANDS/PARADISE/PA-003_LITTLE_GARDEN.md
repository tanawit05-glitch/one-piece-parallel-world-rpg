# OVERVIEW

Little Garden is one of the most dangerous prehistoric islands in Paradise.

Unlike civilized islands, Little Garden has remained almost untouched for centuries. Massive volcanoes, ancient forests, gigantic rivers and countless prehistoric creatures dominate the landscape.

The island's ecosystem follows the laws of survival rather than civilization. Predators roam freely, enormous herbivores migrate across open plains, and violent volcanic activity constantly reshapes the terrain.

Few permanent residents exist. Most visitors are explorers, hunters, researchers, Marines conducting reconnaissance, or pirates seeking glory.

Because the Log Pose requires an exceptionally long time to record the island's magnetic field, many crews are forced to remain far longer than expected, greatly increasing their chances of encountering danger.
