# OVERVIEW

Enies Lobby serves as the primary judicial center of the World Government within Paradise.

Unlike ordinary islands, Enies Lobby functions as a massive governmental complex dedicated to law, criminal prosecution, intelligence coordination and high-security detention transfer.

Every individual arriving at the island is subject to strict inspection and identification procedures.

The island receives judges, prosecutors, investigators, Marine officers and government officials from every region under World Government authority.

Because of its political significance, Enies Lobby maintains some of the highest security standards anywhere in Paradise.

To ordinary citizens it symbolizes justice.

To criminals it represents the final destination before imprisonment.

# CULTURE

## General Culture

Although Enies Lobby is not a traditional civilian city, it possesses a distinct institutional culture built upon discipline, order and absolute professionalism.

Life on the island revolves around schedules, regulations and official responsibilities.

Employees are expected to maintain punctuality, confidentiality and impartiality regardless of their position.

Unlike commercial cities, social status is determined primarily by experience, competence and public service rather than wealth.

---

## Daily Life

Government offices begin operating shortly after sunrise.

Court hearings occupy most of the day.

Administrative departments continue processing reports and legal documents well into the evening.

Marine patrols rotate continuously.

Evidence laboratories, archives and communication centers operate twenty-four hours a day.

Nighttime is noticeably quieter, though security activity never decreases.

# SECRETS

Several areas beneath Enies Lobby predate the establishment of the current judicial complex.

Some historical trials remain permanently classified despite their age.

Hidden emergency evacuation routes exist beneath nearly every government building.

Not every confiscated artifact stored within Evidence Vault Zero has ever been publicly documented.

A number of forgotten investigations were quietly closed without official explanation.

Certain handwritten legal records contain references to government institutions that no longer officially exist.
