# OVERVIEW

Whisky Peak is the first major settlement encountered by many crews after entering Paradise.

To outsiders, the island appears peaceful and welcoming. Music fills the streets, restaurants remain open throughout the night, and visitors are greeted with celebrations. Behind this cheerful façade, however, Whisky Peak has earned a notorious reputation as one of the Grand Line's largest bounty-hunting communities.

Almost every resident has received some form of combat training. Families pass down techniques for tracking, surveillance, and capturing dangerous criminals. Because of this, the island has developed a unique culture where hospitality and suspicion exist side by side.

Although bounty hunting remains the island's primary profession, legal commerce, tourism, ship repair, and information brokerage have also become important industries.
