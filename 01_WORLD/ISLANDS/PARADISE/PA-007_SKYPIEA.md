# OVERVIEW

Skypiea is one of the largest civilizations that exists above the Grand Line.

Located thousands of meters above sea level, the kingdom rests upon enormous cloud formations known collectively as the White Sea.

Unlike the oceans below, transportation depends upon cloud currents, Wind Dial technology, aerial vessels and trained Sky Creatures capable of navigating the ever-changing atmosphere.

The people of Skypiea have developed an entirely different civilization from the Blue Sea, adapting to a world where clouds replace oceans, islands float freely, and weather changes with astonishing speed.

Although isolated from the surface for centuries, Skypiea has become an important center of Dial technology, cloud engineering, weather research and aerial navigation.

Visitors from the Blue Sea are uncommon but not unknown. Most arrive accidentally through Knock Up Streams or other rare atmospheric phenomena.

# SKY ECOSYSTEM

Life above the clouds has evolved differently from the Blue Sea.

Most animals possess lightweight skeletons, wide wings or natural buoyancy.

Cloud Fish swim through dense cloud currents as though they were water.

Vegetation anchors itself into Island Clouds using specialized root systems.

The ecosystem depends heavily upon weather cycles and the seasonal movement of cloud masses.

# SECRETS

Several government archives describe civilizations that existed in the sky long before the current kingdom was founded.

Not every ancient Dial discovered by archaeologists is publicly documented.

Certain experimental Dials possess functions unknown even to modern engineers.

Hidden cloud tunnels connect remote regions of Skypiea without appearing on official maps.

The Weather Observatory secretly monitors atmospheric phenomena that cannot be explained by modern science.

Some floating islands slowly migrate around the Grand Line over periods lasting decades.

Deep beneath the White-White Sea exist cloud formations dense enough to support enormous underground ecosystems.

Several noble families quietly finance expeditions searching for forgotten Dial technology capable of transforming the balance of power across the sky.

# GM NOTES

Recommended Campaign Length

10–20 Sessions

Recommended Player Level

Mid Paradise

Core Themes

- Exploration
- Discovery
- Technology
- Adventure
- Weather
- Navigation
- Ancient Civilization
- Scientific Research
- Aerial Survival

Skypiea should feel completely different from every Blue Sea island.

Travel should emphasize three-dimensional movement through cloud oceans, floating islands and changing wind currents rather than ordinary sailing.

Weather is not simply background scenery.

Storms, cloud currents and atmospheric pressure should actively influence travel, combat, trade and exploration.

Dial technology should be present throughout everyday life, making the kingdom feel technologically unique while remaining faithful to the One Piece world.

Every settlement should possess its own identity, economy and traditions centered around life above the clouds.

Player decisions may permanently affect

- Trade Routes
- Weather Safety
- Public Opinion
- Scientific Discoveries
- Dial Technology
- Government Stability
- Merchant Relationships
- Crime Levels
- Transportation Infrastructure

The White Sea should always inspire wonder.

Players should constantly feel that they are exploring a civilization unlike anything found below the clouds, where the sky itself is both the greatest opportunity and the greatest danger.
