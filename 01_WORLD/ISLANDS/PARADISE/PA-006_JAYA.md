# OVERVIEW

Jaya is one of the most unpredictable islands in Paradise.

Unlike kingdoms governed by centralized authority, Jaya is divided between civilized settlements, lawless pirate towns, dense tropical forests, ancient ruins, and unexplored highlands.

Its strategic position makes it a gathering point for pirates, bounty hunters, explorers, archaeologists, merchants, and adventurers preparing to continue deeper into the Grand Line.

The island is famous for stories of lost civilizations, hidden treasure, mysterious knock-up streams, and legends surrounding the Sky Islands.

Because no single government controls the island, every district has developed according to its own rules.

Violence is common in some regions, while others remain surprisingly peaceful.

# MAJOR REGIONS

## Mock Town

The island's most infamous settlement.

Pirates, bounty hunters, smugglers and mercenaries gather here.

Violence is common.

Despite its reputation, numerous legitimate businesses operate openly.

---

## South Jungle

Dense tropical rainforest.

Filled with dangerous wildlife.

Several forgotten ruins remain hidden beneath centuries of vegetation.

---

## Bell Highlands

Rocky mountains overlooking the island.

Excellent observation points.

Contains abandoned temples and collapsed structures.

---

## Eastern Coast

Fishing villages and merchant docks.

Relatively peaceful compared to Mock Town.

---

## Ancient Ruin Zone

Scattered remains of an unknown civilization.

Many archaeological expeditions have disappeared here.
