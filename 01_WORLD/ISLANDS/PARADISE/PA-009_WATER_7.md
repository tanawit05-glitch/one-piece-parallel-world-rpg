# OVERVIEW

Water 7 is the greatest shipbuilding city in Paradise and one of the most prosperous maritime centers anywhere in the Grand Line.

Built upon a vast network of canals, waterways and artificial islands, the city has adapted to life surrounded by the sea for centuries.

Every district is connected by bridges, locks, water elevators and canals where transportation never truly stops.

The city attracts merchants, engineers, navigators, inventors and adventurers from every corner of the world.

Its reputation rests upon master shipwrights capable of constructing vessels that can survive even the most dangerous seas of the Grand Line.

Despite its wealth and technological advancement, Water 7 constantly struggles against one unstoppable enemy:

The sea itself.

Every year the city prepares for Aqua Laguna, a catastrophic tidal disaster that threatens to destroy everything built by generations before.

# NATURAL PHENOMENA

## Aqua Laguna

The greatest natural disaster affecting Water 7.

Once every year, enormous tidal waves generated somewhere within the Grand Line strike the city with devastating force.

Water levels rise dramatically.

Entire districts may flood.

Transportation completely changes.

Emergency flood barriers are activated throughout the city.

Citizens begin preparations weeks before the event.

Shipyards suspend construction.

Government offices coordinate evacuations and emergency shelters.

Every resident respects Aqua Laguna.

No one underestimates it.

# CULTURE

## General Culture

Water 7 has developed a culture centered around craftsmanship, cooperation and life upon the water.

Every citizen grows up surrounded by canals, bridges and ships.

Engineering is not viewed simply as a profession, but as an art passed from one generation to the next.

Citizens take enormous pride in the city's reputation as the greatest shipbuilding center in Paradise.

Visitors quickly notice that conversations about ships, weather and Aqua Laguna are part of everyday life.

Because international merchants constantly arrive from across the Grand Line, Water 7 has become one of the most culturally diverse cities in Paradise.

# SECRETS

Historical records suggest Water 7 has been rebuilt numerous times after catastrophic flooding.

Several sections of the city's oldest foundations are far older than the current government admits.

Some engineering techniques used by the Shipwright Guild cannot be reproduced by modern craftsmen.

Hidden emergency flood gates exist beneath the city that appear on no official map.

Certain wealthy merchants secretly compete to obtain forgotten shipbuilding blueprints from across the Grand Line.

Engineers have recorded subtle changes in Aqua Laguna for decades, yet no one understands why the tides continue to grow stronger.

Legends claim an unfinished masterpiece vessel still rests somewhere beneath the city, waiting for the right shipwright to complete it.
