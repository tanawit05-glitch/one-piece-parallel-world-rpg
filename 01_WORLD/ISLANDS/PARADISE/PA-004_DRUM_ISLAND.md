# OVERVIEW

Drum Island is a winter kingdom located deep within Paradise and is recognized throughout the Grand Line as one of the world's greatest centers of medical knowledge.

Towering snow-covered mountains dominate the island, while scattered villages survive within valleys protected from freezing winds. Nearly every family has some understanding of medicine, herbal treatment, or survival in extreme climates.

Although life is harsh, the people of Drum Island possess remarkable resilience. Visitors often arrive seeking doctors, rare medicines, or treatment for illnesses that cannot be cured elsewhere.

The island's environment is beautiful but unforgiving. Heavy snowfall, avalanches, blizzards, and dangerous wildlife constantly threaten travelers who venture beyond established roads.
