# OVERVIEW

Reverse Mountain is the only known natural gateway that allows ships from the Four Blues to enter the Grand Line.

Rather than functioning as a traditional island with large civilian populations, Reverse Mountain is a massive continental mountain system located at the intersection of the Red Line and the Grand Line.

Powerful ocean currents flow upward from all four seas before merging at the summit and descending into Paradise.

Because every ship entering the Grand Line must pass through this location, Reverse Mountain has become one of the busiest maritime choke points in the world.

The surrounding region is constantly monitored by the World Government, Marine observers, independent navigators, merchants, bounty hunters, and pirates.

While no permanent kingdom exists here, temporary settlements, maintenance stations, rescue outposts, and supply facilities have gradually formed around the mountain.
