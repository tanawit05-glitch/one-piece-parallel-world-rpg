# OVERVIEW

Moonshell Island is a tranquil island famous for its brilliant white
beaches and rare moon-colored shells that wash ashore during certain
tides. The island thrives on fishing, shellcraft, and coastal trade
while remaining relatively untouched by large-scale conflict.
