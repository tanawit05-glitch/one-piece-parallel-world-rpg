------------------------------------------------------------------------

# OVERVIEW

Gekko Atoll is a ring-shaped coral atoll in East Blue. Calm lagoons lie
inside the reef while the outer waters are notorious for hidden coral
formations that can wreck careless ships. The island supports small
coastal communities that depend on fishing, pearl diving, and coral
harvesting.
