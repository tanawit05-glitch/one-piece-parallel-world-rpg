# EAST BLUE HISTORY

## Parallel World Rule

Canon geography is preserved.
All historical figures are original characters.

---

## Early Era

Fishing settlements gradually expanded into trading ports.

---

## Trade Expansion

Merchant routes linked the major islands and encouraged Marine deployment.

---

## Age of Piracy

Local pirate crews emerged, targeting merchant shipping rather than conquering territory.

---

## Modern Era

East Blue is considered peaceful compared to the Grand Line but still experiences piracy, corruption, smuggling and political conflicts.

---

## GM Usage

History should influence politics, local traditions and rumors without forcing player outcomes.
