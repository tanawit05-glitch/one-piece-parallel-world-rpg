# EAST BLUE PIRATES

## Overview
Most pirate crews are inexperienced and operate locally.

## Typical Crew
- Crew Size: 5–30
- Average Bounty: 3–20 Million
- Devil Fruit Users: Rare
- Haki Users: Extremely Rare

## Activities
- Cargo theft
- Smuggling
- Port extortion
- Kidnapping
- Treasure hunting

## GM Notes
East Blue pirates should feel dangerous to civilians but not world-level threats.
