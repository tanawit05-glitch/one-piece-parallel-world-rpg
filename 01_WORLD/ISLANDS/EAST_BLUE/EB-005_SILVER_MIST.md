Sea: East Blue Classification: Canon Island World Type: One Piece

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# OVERVIEW

Silver Mist Island is a medium-sized island in East Blue surrounded by
dense sea fog throughout most of the year. Its isolated environment has
shaped a self-reliant fishing community and made the island a difficult
destination for pirates and merchants alike.
