# EAST BLUE MARINES

## Purpose
The East Blue Marine Branch is responsible for maintaining order, suppressing piracy, protecting merchant traffic, enforcing World Government law, and responding to emergencies.

---

## Organization

### Regional Headquarters
Coordinates operations across East Blue.

### Local Bases
Located near major ports and populated islands.

### Outposts
Small stations responsible for nearby villages and shipping lanes.

---

## Responsibilities

- Patrol sea lanes
- Escort merchant vessels
- Investigate crimes
- Arrest pirates and bandits
- Disaster relief
- Protect World Government facilities

---

## Patrol Levels

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| I | Harbor Patrol |
| II | Coastal Patrol |
| III | Open Sea Patrol |
| IV | Anti-Pirate Operation |
| V | Emergency Mobilization |

---

## Standard Fleet

### Patrol Boat
Crew: 10–30

### Medium Warship
Crew: 50–120

### Large Battleship
Crew: 150+

---

## Response Priority

1. Civilian distress
2. Pirate attack
3. Marine base emergency
4. Government request
5. Routine patrol

---

## Investigation Procedure

1. Receive report
2. Verify evidence
3. Dispatch patrol
4. Capture suspects
5. Submit report
6. Update bounty if necessary

---

## Relationships

### Civilians
Generally positive.

### Merchants
Strong cooperation.

### Pirates
Hostile.

---

## Corruption

Although Marine doctrine values justice, individual officers may abuse authority. Such corruption should remain local and become story hooks rather than the norm.

---

## GM Guidelines

Use Marines as an active organization rather than stationary NPCs.

- Patrols move between islands.
- Reports spread through Den Den Mushi.
- Pirate actions increase patrol frequency.
- Helping Marines improves reputation.
