# EAST BLUE SEA INFORMATION

## Overview
East Blue is the calmest of the Four Blues. Navigation is simple, weather is stable, piracy exists but is generally limited to small crews, and Marine influence is strong.

## Sea Characteristics
- Navigation Difficulty: Low
- Weather Stability: High
- Sea King Activity: Rare
- Average Wave Height: 1–5 m
- Visibility: Excellent

## Navigation
Uses compass, stars, sea charts and coastline. Log Pose is unnecessary.

## Common Wildlife
- Tuna
- Marlin
- Flying Fish
- Dolphins
- Sea Turtles
- Sharks

## Strategic Role
East Blue supplies food, timber, fish and recruits to the World Government and Marine forces.

## GM Notes
Ideal starting sea for beginner campaigns focused on exploration, local piracy and character growth.
