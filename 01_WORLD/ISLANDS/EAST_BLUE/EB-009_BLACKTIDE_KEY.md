# OVERVIEW

Blacktide Key is a rugged island on the eastern trade routes of East
Blue. Dark volcanic rock gives its shoreline a black appearance, while
dangerous currents discourage inexperienced sailors. The island survives
through mining, fishing, and servicing passing ships.
