# ==================================================

Sea: East Blue Classification: Canon Sea Restaurant World Type: One
Piece Parallel World

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# OVERVIEW

Baratie is a floating sea restaurant operating in East Blue. Built upon
a massive ship, it welcomes travelers, merchants, Marines, bounty
hunters, and pirates alike. Neutrality and hospitality are valued above
politics, though violence against guests is met with swift response.
