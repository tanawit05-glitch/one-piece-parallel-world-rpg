Sea: East Blue Classification: Canon Island World Type: One Piece

# OVERVIEW

Syrup Village is a peaceful rural settlement in East Blue surrounded by
fertile farmland and gentle hills. It is known for its quiet lifestyle,
skilled craftsmen, and agricultural production. Although relatively
safe, merchant traffic occasionally attracts thieves and pirates.
