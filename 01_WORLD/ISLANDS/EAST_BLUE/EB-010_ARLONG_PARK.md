# ==================================================

Sea: East Blue Classification: Canon Location World Type: One Piece
Parallel World

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# OVERVIEW

Arlong Park was once the headquarters of the Arlong Pirates. In the
Parallel World, the site has been reclaimed after the crew's fall. The
remaining structures stand as a reminder of the island's troubled past,
while nearby communities work to rebuild the area into a peaceful
coastal district.
