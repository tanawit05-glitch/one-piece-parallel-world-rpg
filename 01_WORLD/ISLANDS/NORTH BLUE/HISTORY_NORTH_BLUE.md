# NORTH BLUE HISTORY

## Early Era

- Tribal kingdoms and scattered settlements
- Harsh climate forced centralized survival cities

---

## Industrial Expansion Era

- Rise of weapon manufacturing kingdoms
- Formation of military states (Germa-type systems)
- Early trade networks established

---

## Age of Kingdom Conflicts

- Constant wars between noble states
- Military technology accelerated development
- Intelligence networks formed

---

## Modern Era

- Fragile political balance
- Rising black market intelligence systems
- Pirates begin targeting industrial supply chains

---

## GM Core Rule

North Blue history should directly influence:
- Kingdom politics
- Military behavior
- Black market strength
- Player reputation systems
