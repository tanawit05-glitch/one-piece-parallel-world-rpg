# NORTH BLUE PIRATES

## Overview

Pirates in North Blue are more organized and dangerous than East Blue.

---

## Structure Types

- Syndicate Pirates
- Kingdom Rebel Pirates
- Industrial Raiders
- Intelligence Pirates

---

## Crew Size

- 10–80 average
- 100+ for syndicates

---

## Average Bounty

15M – 50M+

---

## Behavior

- Target industrial supply chains
- Steal weapons and technology
- Collaborate with black market networks
- Some act as mercenaries

---
