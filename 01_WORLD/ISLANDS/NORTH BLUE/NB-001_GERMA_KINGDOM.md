# OVERVIEW

Germa Kingdom is a mobile military nation composed of advanced floating territories. Unlike traditional kingdoms, Germa does not maintain a fixed homeland. Instead, it operates as a fleet-based kingdom that can relocate across seas.

The kingdom is built upon extreme scientific advancement, genetic engineering, and military production. It is feared across the world for its highly disciplined clone armies and experimental warfare technology.

Germa rarely engages in diplomacy. Its presence in any sea is treated as a strategic military event.

# STRUCTURE OF GERMA

Germa Kingdom is composed of multiple “Mobile Sub-Kingdoms” called **FLOATING UNITS**

## Floating Units

- Command Core Vessel (Central HQ)
- Research Vessel
- Infantry Production Vessel
- Weapons Development Vessel
- Logistics Vessel
- Medical & Bio Lab Vessel
- Training Vessel

Each vessel can operate independently but is synchronized under a central command system.
