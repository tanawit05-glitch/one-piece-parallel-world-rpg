# OVERVIEW

Crow Island is a hidden intelligence hub shrouded in permanent twilight fog, known only through fragmented maps and pirate rumors.

Unlike conventional islands, Crow Island does not appear consistently on navigation charts. Its location seems to shift slightly depending on weather, tides, and unknown environmental factors.

It is widely believed to be the central meeting point for illegal information brokers across North Blue.
