# OVERVIEW

Winter Heart is a massive frozen continental region located in the far north of North Blue. Unlike typical winter islands, this area is permanently locked in a deep freeze that never thaws, regardless of seasonal changes.

The region is considered one of the most stable yet hostile cold environments in the world, combining extreme survival conditions with high political and military importance.

It is often referred to as “The Frozen Crown of the North Blue.”
