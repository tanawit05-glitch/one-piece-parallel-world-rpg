# OVERVIEW

Howling Bay is a massive semi-natural harbor surrounded by jagged cliffs and deep sea caves. The name comes from the constant “howling sound” produced by wind passing through the cliff tunnels and broken shipwreck structures.

It is one of the most chaotic maritime hubs in North Blue, functioning as a neutral ground where pirates, smugglers, mercenaries, and illegal merchants trade freely under unstable local control.

Unlike official ports, Howling Bay has no unified authority.
