# OVERVIEW

Frozen Spire is a colossal vertical ice structure rising far beyond natural mountain heights. It is considered one of the most mysterious geological formations in the world, with theories suggesting it may not be entirely natural.

The Spire is surrounded by frozen wastelands and impossible weather systems, making approach extremely dangerous.

Many believe it is a “sealed structure” hiding something at its core.
