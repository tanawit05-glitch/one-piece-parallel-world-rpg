# OVERVIEW

Lvneel Kingdom is a vast continental-style monarchy located in the colder regions of North Blue. Unlike mobile kingdoms or maritime nations, Lvneel is a deeply rooted land empire with centuries of political structure, noble lineage, and rigid class hierarchy.

It is known for its strict aristocratic system, powerful knight orders, and internal political tension between royal authority and regional noble houses.

Lvneel is currently in a fragile “peace period” that hides long-term structural instability.
