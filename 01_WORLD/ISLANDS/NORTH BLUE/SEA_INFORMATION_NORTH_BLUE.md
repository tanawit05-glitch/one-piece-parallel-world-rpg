# NORTH BLUE SEA INFORMATION

## Overview

North Blue is a semi-hostile, semi-developed sea with strong kingdoms, industrial economies, and rising political tension.

---

## Sea Characteristics

- Navigation Difficulty: Medium
- Weather Stability: Medium-Low
- Sea Kings: Rare but dangerous
- Political Conflict: High
- Industrial Activity: Very High

---

## Key Features

- Kingdom-based world structure
- Industrial mega-cities exist
- Intelligence networks active
- Pirate syndicates organized
- Black market deeply integrated

---

## GM Core Identity

North Blue is NOT a beginner sea.

It is:

- Mid-tier campaign zone
- Political + industrial sandbox
- Espionage-heavy environment
- Preparation zone for Grand Line escalation
