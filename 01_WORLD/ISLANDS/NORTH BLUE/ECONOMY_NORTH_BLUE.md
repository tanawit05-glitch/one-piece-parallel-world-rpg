# NORTH BLUE ECONOMY SYSTEM

## Main Exports

- Iron & Steel
- Ship components
- Weapons
- Industrial goods
- Rare minerals
- Medical research products

---

## Imports

- Luxury goods
- Advanced navigation tools
- Exotic materials
- High-end medicine
- Foreign technology

---

## Economic Structure

- Kingdom-based economies dominate
- Industrial hubs (Steelport style cities)
- Merchant guild monopolies
- Black market influence is high

---

## Currency

Standard Belly economy  
+ emerging credit systems in industrial zones

---

## Key Difference from East Blue

- Trade is industrial, not agricultural
- Weapons trade is major economy driver
- Economic warfare exists between kingdoms
