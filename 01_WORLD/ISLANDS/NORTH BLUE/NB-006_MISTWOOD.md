# OVERVIEW

Mistwood is a vast forest island permanently covered in dense, shifting fog. Visibility is extremely limited, and even experienced navigators often lose direction within minutes of entering the forest.

The island is considered a “Living Navigation Trap” due to unpredictable terrain shifts and unnatural fog behavior that interferes with all known navigation systems.

Many who enter Mistwood never return.
