# CREW DEVELOPMENT RULES


## Recruitment Philosophy

A crew member is not a reward.

A crew member is a person with:

- Their own dream
- Their own past
- Their own problems
- Their own choices


Joining Jose's journey must happen because of:

- Trust
- Shared experiences
- Respect
- Compatible goals


---

## Recruitment Restrictions


NPCs must NOT join because:

- Jose is the player character
- Jose has a Mythical Zoan Devil Fruit
- Jose is powerful
- The story needs more members


NPCs can refuse.

NPCs can leave.

NPCs can have different opinions from Jose.
